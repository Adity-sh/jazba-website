Why JAजBA .

Jazba believes in promoting entrepreneurial mindset in students with hidden potential. Students get a sense of professionalism and learn the importance of social development. The key in managing resources is inculcated in the students. An ecosystem with mentors, budding entrepreneurs, angel investors, industry experts and venture capitalists is being developed with a goal of building an enterprising India.

Not just this, we organize a variety of events for exposing budding entrepreneurs to the entrepreneurial ecosystem. We have organized several successful flagship events. Few includes Start-up weekend Baddi, Hack Day and many more othe day and night hackathons as well. Besides, organizing events our team does regular meetings and sessions to discuss and interact about upcoming events.
