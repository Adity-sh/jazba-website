One-to-many
Brainstorming Session

Event Date March 2nd, 2021
Type Entrepreneurial leadership
LEADERSHIP IS THE CAPACITY TO TRANSLATE VISION INTO REALITY

Jazba E cell core of Chitkara University Himachal Pradesh organized a one-to-many session with all the Hack Day participants and E Cell members A brainstorm session was organized where all the members took part. During the session we took all the reviews and feedback in form of slack of tree with all their expectation from the Jazba E Cell CU HP.



The main vision behind this slack of tree was to get everyone’s expectation and work accordingly in a such a manner that we can fulfil the market and deliver the things as per need. There were some mix expectations some were unrealistic and realistic. Based on those the future projects of E-Cell and the way of working will go accordingly based on that. Apart from this many students took part and put their expectation. They were extremely joyful being a part of the session and moving forward in this. By the end of the day everyone felt productive and interactive.
